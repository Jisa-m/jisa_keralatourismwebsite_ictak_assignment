About District

Palakkad is the largest district in the state of Kerala, it is also known as the Granary of Kerala and Rice bowl of Kerala. It is famous for itâ€™s historic Palakkad Fort and Jain Temple, annual Ratholsavam, and beautiful and lushy, Malampuzha Garden and Dam.Palakkad is one of the fourteen districts of Kerala and has no coastal line. The district opens the state to the rest of the country through the Palakkad Gap with a width of 32 to 40 Kms. Its geographical position, historical background, educational status, tourism hot-spots and above all, the development activities that are carried out, are wide and varied. The district is one of the main granaries of Kerala and its economy is primarily agricultural. The district is also the land of Palmyrahs.
